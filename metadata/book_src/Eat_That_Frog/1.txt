
Introduction:

Brian Tracy's book  Eat That Frog! 21 Great Ways to Stop Procrastinating and Get More Done in Less Time  is a practical guide to overcoming procrastination and improving productivity. The book's title is a metaphor for tackling your most challenging and important tasks—the  frogs —first thing in the morning, ensuring that you accomplish your priorities before anything else.

Tracy draws upon his extensive experience as a productivity expert and provides readers with actionable strategies, techniques, and principles to boost their efficiency, time management, and goal attainment. The book is designed to help individuals at all levels of their careers, from students to professionals, make better use of their time, reduce stress, and achieve greater success.
