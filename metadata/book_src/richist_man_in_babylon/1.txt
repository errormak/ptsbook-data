 The Richest Man in Babylon  by George S. Clason

In the introduction of the book, George S. Clason sets the stage by describing the historical context of Babylon, a city known for its wealth and prosperity. 
He explains that the principles of financial success explored in the book are derived from ancient Babylonian parables and lessons, 
which have been passed down through generations.

Clason introduces the reader to the characters and stories that will convey these timeless principles. 
He emphasizes that the secrets to wealth, as taught in Babylon, are not reserved for the privileged few but are available to anyone who seeks them. 
The introduction invites readers to journey back in time to learn the wisdom that made Babylon the wealthiest city of its era.
