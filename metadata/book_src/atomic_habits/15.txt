Episode 14: The Law of the Few

Clear continues to explore the laws of behavior change, focusing on the  Law of the Few  in this episode. 
He discusses the role of social influence and the impact of the people around us on our habits. Clear explains that certain individuals, known as  key influencers,  
can play a significant role in shaping our behaviors.

Clear introduces the concept of  social norms  and how they affect our actions. He emphasizes the importance of choosing the right social environment 
to support positive habits and how the power of peer pressure can be harnessed to reinforce desired behaviors.
