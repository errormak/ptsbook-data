Episode 1: The Surprising Power of Atomic Habits

In this foundational episode, James Clear introduces the central theme of  Atomic Habits.  He explains that small, incremental changes, or  atomic habits,  
can lead to significant personal transformation over time. Clear emphasizes the compounding effect of these habits and how they shape our identities and outcomes.

Clear discusses the importance of understanding the difference between goals and systems, highlighting that habits are the systems that drive progress.
 He shares a compelling story of British cycling's transformation, showing how tiny improvements in various areas led to their unprecedented success.
