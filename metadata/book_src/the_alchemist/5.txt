Episode 4: Santiago's Job in the Crystal Shop 

Desperate to continue his journey, Santiago finds employment at a crystal shop in Tangier. 
Initially resentful of being stuck in a mundane job, Santiago soon realizes that every experience holds lessons. He learns about patience and perseverance,
virtues that are essential for any journey.

During his time at the crystal shop, Santiago discovers the concept of the  language of the world.  
This universal language reveals itself when he listens to his heart and pays attention to the signs and omens that guide him. Santiago's 
experiences at the crystal shop reaffirm his commitment to his Personal Legend and deepen his understanding of the transformative power of following one's dreams.
