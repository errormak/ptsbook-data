Part One: Experiences in a Concentration Camp

Episode 1: Arrival at Auschwitz

In this episode, we delve into Viktor Frankl's poignant account of his arrival at Auschwitz, one of the most notorious concentration camps of the Holocaust. This was a moment of profound shock and disorientation, as he was stripped of his identity and possessions, reduced to a mere number. Frankl's recollection offers a chilling glimpse into the dehumanizing reality that prisoners faced in these camps. We explore the initial shock and confusion, setting the stage for the profound insights that would emerge from his experiences.
