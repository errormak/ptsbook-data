Episode 2: The Struggle for Survival
Within the unforgiving confines of Auschwitz, Frankl and his fellow prisoners faced daily struggles for survival. In this episode, we delve into the harsh conditions, inadequate nutrition, and constant threat of violence that characterized life in the camp. Frankl's observations highlight the extraordinary lengths to which individuals can adapt and endure, driven by their innate will to live. We explore the resilience of the human spirit as prisoners clung to hope, seeking meaning even in the face of unimaginable suffering.
