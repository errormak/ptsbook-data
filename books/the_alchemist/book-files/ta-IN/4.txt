Episode 3: Santiago's Arrival in Tangier 

Santiago's journey takes him to the bustling city of Tangier in Morocco. Here, he encounters a vastly different world from the tranquil hills of Andalusia. 
Santiago's initial optimism is tested when he becomes a victim of theft, losing all his money. This setback could have discouraged him, 
but Santiago remains determined to pursue his dream.

Through this experience, Santiago learns the importance of discernment and not placing blind trust in everyone he meets.
 He begins to understand that the journey towards one's Personal Legend is not always easy and may involve unexpected challenges. 
Yet, he refuses to waver in his resolve to continue his quest.
