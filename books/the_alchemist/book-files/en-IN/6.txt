Episode 5: Santiago's Meeting with the Englishman 

In the second part of  The Alchemist,  Santiago continues his journey through the desert. 
He meets an Englishman who is on his own quest to find the alchemist a legendary figure who possesses the secret of turning base metals into gold and the elixir of life.
 The Englishman is a scholar, deeply engrossed in books and theories. Santiago, on the other hand, believes in the power of experience and listening to the heart. 
Their encounter sparks a fascinating discussion about different approaches to knowledge and the pursuit of dreams.
