Episode 1: Santiago the Shepherd

 The Alchemist  opens in the rolling hills of Andalusia, Spain, where we meet Santiago, a young shepherd boy. 
Santiago is content with his simple life, tending to his sheep. However, he's haunted by a recurring dream a dream of discovering treasure at the base of the 
Egyptian pyramids. This dream fills him with a sense of wonder and purpose, urging him to embark on a journey beyond the familiar.

Santiago's decision to follow this dream marks the beginning of his adventure. It's a decision that carries both excitement and uncertainty.
 He knows he must sell his sheep, which represent his comfort zone, to fund this quest. Santiago's courage to pursue his dream, despite the uncertainty, 
sets the stage for a profound journey of self-discovery and realization.
