Episode 15: The Alchemist's Departure 

The time comes for the alchemist to leave Santiago and return to his own path. Santiago is grateful for the guidance and wisdom the alchemist has shared with him. 
The alchemist reminds Santiago that the journey is never truly over, as life is a continuous process of growth and discovery.

Before parting, the alchemist imparts one final lesson: that the universe will always conspire to help those who pursue their dreams. 
He tells Santiago that he has all the knowledge he needs within himself and that he must continue to listen to his heart and follow the omens. 
Santiago is left with a sense of empowerment and a renewed commitment to his Personal Legend.
