Episode 14: The Return to the Oasis 

After their unsuccessful search for treasure at the pyramids, Santiago and the alchemist return to the oasis. 
Santiago is filled with mixed emotions, but he also feels a sense of peace and acceptance. 
He reunites with Fatima, the love he met at the oasis, and they share a deep connection that transcends words.

Santiago learns from the alchemist that love is a part of the Soul of the World and that it can be a powerful force in achieving one's Personal Legend. 
He understands that his love for Fatima is an integral part of his journey, and he is willing to continue following his heart, 
even if it means leaving her behind temporarily.
