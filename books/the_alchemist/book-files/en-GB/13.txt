Episode 12: The Final Test 

Santiago and the alchemist draw closer to their destination the Egyptian pyramids, where Santiago believes his treasure awaits.
 However, their journey takes an unexpected turn when they are captured by a group of hostile tribesmen. Santiago faces a life-threatening test of his 
faith and determination as he confronts the possibility of losing everything he has worked for. It is in this moment of crisis that Santiago realizes the 
true nature of his treasure and the significance of his journey.
