Episode 8: The Desert's Lessons 

Under the guidance of the alchemist, Santiago embarks on a perilous journey into the desert. Here, he encounters various challenges and tests, 
both physical and spiritual. The desert represents the ultimate trial, a place where only those who truly believe in their dreams can survive. 
Santiago learns valuable lessons about facing fear, trusting his intuition, and understanding the power of the Soul of the World. 
As he approaches the Egyptian pyramids, his destination, Santiago is poised to realize the true nature of his treasure and the depth of his own transformation.
