Episode 16: Santiago's Return to Spain

With the guidance of the alchemist's teachings and the love of Fatima in his heart, Santiago returns to Spain. He is a changed man, 
carrying the wisdom of the desert and the knowledge that the journey itself is the true treasure. Santiago reunites with the old fortune-teller and the crystal merchant,
 who have their own realizations about the significance of their lives.

As Santiago contemplates his future, he understands that he has fulfilled his Personal Legend, and his journey has come full circle. 
He is no longer a shepherd boy, but a true alchemist of his own destiny, forever transformed by the lessons of the desert.
