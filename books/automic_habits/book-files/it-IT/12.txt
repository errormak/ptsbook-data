Episode 11: Advanced Tactics: How to Go from Being Merely Good to Being Truly Great

In Part 6, Clear delves into advanced strategies for habit development and personal improvement. He explores the concept of setting "stretch goals," 
which are ambitious targets that challenge individuals to reach new levels of performance. Stretch goals push individuals beyond their comfort zones 
and encourage continuous growth.

Clear also discusses the concept of "deliberate practice," a structured approach to skill development that involves focused, intentional effort. 
He emphasizes the importance of feedback and incremental improvements in the pursuit of mastery.
