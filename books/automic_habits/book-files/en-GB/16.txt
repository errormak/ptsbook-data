Episode 15: The Law of Inverse Relationships

In Part 8, Clear explores the concept of the "Law of Inverse Relationships." He discusses how our behaviors and choices often involve trade-offs. 
Achieving greatness in one area of life may require sacrifices in another. Clear encourages readers to consider their priorities and be intentional 
about where they invest their time and energy.

Clear also introduces the idea of "time management" as a form of attention management. He emphasizes the importance of focusing on 
high-impact activities and eliminating distractions to make the most of limited time and resources.
