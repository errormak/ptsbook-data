Episode 4: The Best Way to Start a New Habit

In this episode, Clear explores the key to starting new habits: making them as easy as possible. He introduces the concept of "habit shaping," 
which involves breaking down habits into smaller, manageable steps. By reducing friction and resistance, we increase the likelihood of success.

Clear also touches on the importance of environment design in shaping habits. Creating an environment that supports desired behaviors can 
have a profound impact on habit formation. He provides practical tips for optimizing your surroundings to reinforce positive habits.
