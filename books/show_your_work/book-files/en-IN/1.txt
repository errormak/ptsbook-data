Introduction:

In the introduction of  Show Your Work!,  Austin Kleon sets the stage for the book by addressing a common challenge faced by many creative individuals: the struggle to gain visibility and recognition for their work. He acknowledges that in today's digital age, getting discovered is essential for creative success, and he introduces the idea that sharing one's creative process is a powerful way to achieve this.

Kleon emphasizes that sharing doesn't have to be intimidating or self-promotional but can be a genuine and generous act. He encourages readers to open up about their creative journey, showing the steps, influences, and discoveries that lead to their work. The introduction invites readers to embrace the concept of sharing their creativity with the world and provides a preview of the ten principles that will be explored in the book.
