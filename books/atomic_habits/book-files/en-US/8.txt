Episode 7: The 3rd Law: Make It Easy

In the fourth part of the book, Clear introduces the third law of behavior change:  Make It Easy.  He argues that simplicity is key to habit formation. 
Making good habits easy to start and hard habits difficult to continue is a fundamental strategy for success.

Clear discusses the concept of  habit friction  and how to reduce it to make desirable behaviors effortless. He also explores the role of environment 
in shaping habits and provides insights on how to optimize your surroundings for better habit formation.
