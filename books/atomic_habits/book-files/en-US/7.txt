Episode 6: How to Find and Fix the Causes of Your Bad Habits

In this episode, Clear delves into the mechanics of identifying and addressing the root causes of bad habits. 
He introduces the concept of the  habit loop,  which consists of cue, craving, response, and reward. Understanding this loop helps in pinpointing 
the triggers and rewards associated with negative behaviors.

Clear emphasizes the importance of self-awareness and tracking your habits to uncover hidden patterns. He provides strategies for breaking down the 
habit loop and disrupting the cycle of undesirable behaviors. By addressing the underlying causes, individuals can effectively replace bad habits 
with more constructive ones.
