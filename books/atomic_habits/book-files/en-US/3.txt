Episode 2: How Your Habits Shape Your Identity (and Vice Versa)

Clear delves into the deep connection between habits and identity. He argues that our identity emerges from our habits our actions reinforce our beliefs about ourselves.
 To change our habits, we must first change our self-image.

Clear introduces the concept of  the two-minute rule,  a strategy for initiating new habits by starting with small, easy actions. 
He explains that mastering the art of showing up is often more crucial than the magnitude of the effort. Clear's insights in this episode lay the 
groundwork for understanding how habits and identity are intertwined.
