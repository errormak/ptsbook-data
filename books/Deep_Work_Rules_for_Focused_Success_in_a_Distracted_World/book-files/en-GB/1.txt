Newport argues that the ability to concentrate without distraction on demanding tasks is becoming increasingly rare and valuable in our digital, fast-paced world. He introduces the concept of  deep work  as the key to achieving peak productivity, creativity, and success.

Newport begins by highlighting the growing prevalence of shallow work, which consists of low-value tasks like responding to emails, social media, and other distractions that fragment our attention.
