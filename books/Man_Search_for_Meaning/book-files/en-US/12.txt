Episode 11: Understanding Tragic Optimism

In this episode, we delve into the core concept of  tragic optimism.  Frankl proposes that individuals can maintain hope and a sense of meaning even when confronted with extreme suffering or tragedy. Tragic optimism is the belief that life's challenges, no matter how severe, can be imbued with meaning and purpose. We explore how Frankl's experiences in the concentration camps led him to develop this profound concept.
