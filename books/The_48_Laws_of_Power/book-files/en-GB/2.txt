Part 1: The First Set of Laws: The Fundamental Laws of Power

Episode 1: Law 1 - Never Outshine the Master

Law 1 advises against appearing more capable or successful than your superiors or those in positions of authority. Greene explains that by subtly downplaying your achievements, you can avoid jealousy and resentment from those above you. The episode explores real-life examples and scenarios where this law applies, emphasizing the importance of understanding social dynamics in the pursuit of power.
